View of Haarlem from the Northwest, with the Bleaching Fields in the Foreground

Jacob Isaacksz. van Ruisdael - c.1650 / c.1682

oil on canvas - 43cm x 38cm

Foreigners experience the flat Dutch landscape as having a straight, low horizon extending under a vast sky with billowing cumulus clouds.
